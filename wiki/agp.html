AGP is a poorly-made RPG game. It focuses on fighting enemies and defeating areas.

Players start with 50 currencies. Rocks and RBU are secondary currencies. The game also contains a leveling system.

Early-game Gameplay

Players start with only the ability to fight enemies. After an enemy is killed, the player gains currencies and XP. They also gain the ability to go into:

  • Explore Areas - Areas are similar to fighting enemies, but the player does not need to wait for enemies to appear, and they contain multiple enemies. Similar to a "story mode".
  • The Shop - A shop where the player can buy permanent upgrades, upgrade their sword and buy a shield.
  • Gain RBU - The place to gain RBU and buy RBs. RBs contain Buffs, which give buffs to the player, ranging from giving a simple attack buff to giving a chance to heal every turn.
