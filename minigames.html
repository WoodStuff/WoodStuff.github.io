RNG Score Game

A RNG game where each game lasts like 10 seconds at most, average playthroughs last a fraction of a second.
To gain points, risk ending the game every turn. The more you risk, the more points you get. Broken in the sense of just
spamming 1 point turns is the strategy that gives on average the most points (and is the most tedious).
